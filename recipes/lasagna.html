Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Minced beef
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Mash the tomatoes
  3. Mix and cook the tomatoes and the minced beef for 10 minutes with the oil
  4. Staple the ingredients with a layer of noodles in between
  5. Put the cheese on top
  6. Put the form in the oven and bake it for 30 minutes
