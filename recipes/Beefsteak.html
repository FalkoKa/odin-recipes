Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Potatoes

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Put in the butter
  3. Grill the steak for 2 minutes on max heat each side
  4. Cook and peal the Potatoes
  5. Make garlic butter and put it on top of the steak - finish!
